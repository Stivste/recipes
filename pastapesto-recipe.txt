Pasta with Pesto.

Ingredients:
- 80g. wholewheat pasta (rigatoni)
- 40g. pesto (Sacla')
- 10g. grated pecorino cheese

Preparation:
- Boil ~400 mLs of water in a pot
- Once boiling, add pasta and a tea spoon of salt
- Stir from time to time
- wait ~12 minutes for pasta to be ready
- drain the water from the pot while keeping the pasta in it
- add pesto and stir (switch of the cooker)

Serving:
- put in a dish
- add cheese

Et voila'.
